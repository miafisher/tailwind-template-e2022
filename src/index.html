Creating Cotton

The font is created using the pen tool in illustrator. First thing I did was to write down some letters on a piece of paper and then I picked the once that would fit with my concept the best. Next I added a picture of the letters and used the pen tool in AI to draw over them. To finish the letters I added a thicker stroke and a curved width.

Using Cotton

It is an elegant and light font that is very rounded which gives is a soft look, hence the inspiration for the name “Cotton”. The font is best used for headlines or short quotes when you want a minimalistic aesthetic in your design.
